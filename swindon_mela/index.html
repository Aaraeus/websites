Swindon Mela

Swindon Mela is an annual one-day event to celebrate Indian culture in Swindon.

Community

Swindon Mela invites people from all faiths and ethnic backgrounds to join!

Outdoors

The Mela is held outdoors with plenty of community offerings for the whole family.
